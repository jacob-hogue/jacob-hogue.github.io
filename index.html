About me

I'm a Geospatial Solutions Engineer and Conservation Biologist from Lunenburg, Massachusetts, working in the geospatial air mobility domain. I enjoy turning complex geospatial problems into easily-digestible maps, web apps, and visualizations.

My overall objective is to solve geospatial problems for my clients and evangelize the use of geospatial technology for solving complex environmental, social justice, and mobility problems.
